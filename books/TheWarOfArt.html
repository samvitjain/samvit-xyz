The War of Art – Steven Pressfield


- It may be that human race is not ready for freedom. The air of liberty may be too rarefied for us to breathe. Certainly I wound’t be writing this book, on this subject, if living with freedom were easy. The paradox seems to be, as Socrates demonstrated long ago, that the truly free individual is only free to the extent of his own self-mastery. While those who will not govern themselves are condemed to find masters to govern over them.
